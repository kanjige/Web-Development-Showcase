About Me

Raised in a relatively small house in a family of four, my younger years outside of school, extracurriculars, and the Sunday swimming classes I dreaded every week was occupied with one thing in mind: Pokemon. Watching the show, collecting the cards, and playing the same game (Platinum) multiple times naturally led to me wanting to help develop the next game, bringing me down a pipeline of learning about game development and later in my life, programming, leading to me making this website right now.

Since then, I’ve gained interests in UX/UI design, psychology and human interaction, web development and software development as a whole.


Interests

Music

Ever since learning classical instruments in Grade 7 music class and band, I’ve had a strong interest in playing and creating music (albeit very poorly). Instrument wise, I can play the piano and flute to a… passable degree and try to practice the former when I have free time. I aim to one day make a song that I’m proud of and willing to share to people.

Listening wise, I find myself listening to the same artists on loop a bit too much (people that know me personally may call me deranged for it). Currently, those artists include Eve, NewJeans, Natori, and BoyWithUke.

Reading/Writing

Another prevelant hobby I picked up is analyzing and discussing stories, primarily the characters and themes within those stories. I’ve been known to be a bit too analytical over stories I care a lot about, and find myself theorizing and psychoanalyzing characters more than once for my own curiosity. I currently am also writing a story on and off with my random bursts of inspiration.

Other Interests

Other things I find fun is gaming, drawing, and just spending time with friends and family. I also have dreams of owning a coffee shop one day (I don’t drink coffee).
