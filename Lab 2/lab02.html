Step 1: Meditation

Meditation can help improve one’s mindset and how they approach aspects of their lives. It is a simple and free method that would help one achieve their inner Confucius, along with providing additional physical and mental health benefits. By allocating even 5-10 minutes a day to meditation, one would be able to find inner peace and their own Confucius.

To know more about his life and philosophies, reading is a cost effective, accessible way to learn more about the Chinese philosopher. The best text to start off with is The Analects, an ancient Chinese text traditionally believed to be compiled by Confucius' followers.
